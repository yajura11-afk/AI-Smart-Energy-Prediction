Current Findings/Observations: XGBoost outperformed the other regression models in predicting household energy consumption. 
Temperature, hour of the day, and household characteristics showed significant influence on energy consumption. 
An AI-based optimization module was developed to provide personalized energy-saving recommendations and estimate potential energy and cost savings.

Challenges Faced: Handling timestamp feature engineering, selecting the most suitable regression model, reducing GridSearchCV execution time on a large dataset, 
and designing a realistic AI-based optimization module instead of using a simple fixed reduction approach.

Next Steps/Planned Improvements: Develop a Streamlit web application for real-time prediction, prepare the research paper and presentation, enhance the optimization 
module further, and explore advanced models such as LSTM or renewable energy integration in future work.
